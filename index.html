Some details about Wolverine

  • Wolverine is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics, mostly in association with the X-Men.
  • Wolverine (born James "Jimmy" Howlett), also known as Logan, was a mutant born with retractable forearm claws, enhanced physical parameters, and a powerful healing factor.
  • The co-leader of the X-Men, he was also a famous hero and warrior being a veteran of multiple wars.
  • After leading the X-Men for many years and earning a legendary reputation, Logan became old and adult due to poisoning of his admantium skeleton, resulting his healing factors failing.
  • With taking care of Charles Xavier with the help of Caliban, Logan found a "daughter" in X-23, causing Zander Rice and his men to pursue them, until they defeated them both including his own clone X-24, which murdered him gruesomely.
  • Accepting his fate which he always wanted, Wolverine died in peace from his wounds while X-23 next to him.
